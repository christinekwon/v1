tigerride

Tigerride is a web and mobile application that helps students find other students to share rides to and from airports.


At the beginning and end of every break, numerous Princeton students look for people to split Uber/Lyft rides with to nearby airports, because the costs are quite expensive. This website, which I developed with a team of three fellow students, matches people who are looking to travel to the same destination around the same time frame. I was mostly in charge of the front-end development.
