toki bluetooth app

Say hello to Toki!

Toki is an educational robot developed by Hancom Robotics, where I interned the summer after my sophomore year. It teaches English to pre-school and elementary school students through verbal dialogue exchanges and quizzes. It is used at home for daily conversation practice, as well as in schools and English academies.


A frustrating problem that we encountered when testing Toki at various academies was that in classroom environments where there were numerous students conversing with each other at once, Toki often had a difficult time discerning the voice of the student that it was speaking with. After much contemplation and discussion, we eventually decided to implement the functionality to connect a bluetooth headset with Toki so that students can speak into a microphone. This would allow Toki to pick up the individual student's voice better, and the student would also be able to hear Toki more clearly when there is a lot of background noise.


I utilized Android Studio to develop an application for the robot that has the following features:

1. Turn bluetooth functionality on and off
2. Make Toki discoverable to nearby bluetooth devices
3. Display all nearby bluetooth headsets
4. Display all paired devices
5. Allow the user to pair and connect to nearby devices
6. Allow the user to connect to already paired devices
7. Disconnect with devices
8. Unpair with devices
