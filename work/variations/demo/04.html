working together
david whyte
1996


We shape our self
to fit this world


and by the world
are shaped again.


The visible
and the invisible


working together
in common cause,


to produce
the miraculous.


I am thinking of the way
the intangible air


passed at speed
round a shaped wing


easily
holds our weight.


So may we, in this life
trust


to those elements
we have yet to see


or imagine,
and look for the true


shape of our own self,
by forming it well


to the great
intangibles about us.
